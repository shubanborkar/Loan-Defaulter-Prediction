Overview

LoanWise provides a predictive model to assess the risk of loan defaults based on various factors.

Using 23 variables, we developed a regression model to predict a customer's default likelihood.

The dataset contains information from over 30,000 customers in Taiwan. The currency used is Taiwan Dollar (TN$).
